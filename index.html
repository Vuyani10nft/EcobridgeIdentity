Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)

EcoBridge EC collects and processes your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

By providing your information, you consent to EcoBridge EC collecting, storing, and using your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Identity Verification: To create and maintain your BridgeID profile
  • Financial Assessment: To calculate your EcoScore and assess creditworthiness
  • Lender Matching: To connect you with appropriate financial service providers
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our products and services
  • Compliance: To meet regulatory and legal obligations

Your rights under POPIA include:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time
  • The right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

For more information, please read our full Privacy Policy.
